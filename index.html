About Me

I am currently a postdoc at the Morningside Center of Mathematics, Beijing, China. My postdoc advisor is Xin Wan. I am interested in algebraic geometry and number theory. I focus on Iwasawa theory, elliptic curves, and modular forms. I use tools from Galois cohomology to study the size of arithmetic objects. I obtained my math phD at Michigan State University in Spring 2025. My phD advisor is Preston Wake. Here is my CV.
